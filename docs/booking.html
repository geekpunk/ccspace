event booking:

Promoters:

In order to book events at Charm City Art Space you must first become a member. See the membership page for more information on that. You must be a member for at least one month and help staff at least two shows before you can book your first show (one doing door, one doing sound/working the PA). This is so you can get an idea for how shows are run at CCAS before jumping into doing your own shows. All are welcome and we hope to cover a diverse scope of genres inside our walls, so please consider becoming a member and taking part in our space.

Bands:

If you would like a show at Charm City Space you should send an email to charmcityartspace@gmail.com with a note stating the date(s) you're requesting and a link to recorded material. We will forward your request to our promoters at CCAS. Please keep in mind that this is not a full-time venue and it is run by volunteers who book shows and bands they are interested in. No one does this to make money off of it so no one needs to book your band. We will do our best to help you out, but we can not make any guarantees. Shows fill up quickly, so get your requests in as early as possible.

If you are a local band looking to hop on a few shows, check out our upcoming events and see what shows are still looking for bands. If you see one you're interested in playing, you can email the promoter of that show to see if you can be added. The promoters' emails are linked to their names, which are listed next to the date of each show. In addition, please feel free to email us to be considered for future shows.

NOTICE: DUE TO UNFORSEEN CIRCUMSTANCES, THE CHARM CITY ART SPACE HAS BEEN FORCED TO MOVE OUT OF OUR CURRENT LOCATION. AS SUCH, WE DO NOT CURRENTLY HAVE A SPACE, AND WILL NOT BE BOOKING SHOWS UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE. DO NOT EMAIL US, AS WE ARE NO LONGER BOOKING SHOWS. THANK YOU.
