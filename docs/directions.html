Charm City Art Space held its last show in November of 2015. If you would like to contribute to a community arts space in Baltimore, please consider The Undercroft.
directions:

from north of the city:

- take 95 south to 695 west/Towson. then take I-83 south towards Baltimore. take the exit for St. Paul St, and stay right onto Mt. Royal Ave. make your first right onto Charles St, go over the bridge, then turn left at the 2nd light onto Lafayette St. take a left at the first light onto Maryland Ave, and we're the second building on the left, in the middle of the block. you can park on the bridge but keep an eye out for the signs pointing out the proper parking areas and times.

from south of the city:

- take I-95 north to exit 53 for I-395/downtown. take the left fork for downtown. turn right onto Pratt St. (right after the Convention Center). go about 7 or 8 lights and turn left onto President St. this will turn into I-83 north. take that to the North Ave exit (be sure to take the first right fork for North Ave.). turn left off the exit. turn right at the 2nd light onto Maryland Ave. the Space will be on your left just past the first light.

from west of the city:

- take I-70 east all the way to the end. get on 695 going north towards Towson. take the exit for I-83 south towards Baltimore. take the exit for St. Paul St, and stay right onto Mt. Royal Ave. make your first right onto Charles St, go over the bridge, then turn left at the 2nd light onto Lafayette St. take a left at the first light onto Maryland Ave, and we're the second building on the left, in the middle of the block. you can park on the bridge but keep an eye out for the signs pointing out the proper parking areas and times.

using the Light Rail:

- take the Light Rail to the Mt Royal Ave. stop. from there you would head east on Mt Royal (towards the Lyric Opera house), then make a left on Maryland Ave, go over the bridge, and it's on the first block on your right. it's about 5 blocks total.
